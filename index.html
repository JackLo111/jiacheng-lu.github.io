About关于

I'm a zero-year PhD student in Computer Science at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, enrolled in the Zhiyuan Honors Program. My research spans watermarking and safety for large language models, robustness and uncertainty in model editing, and multi-modal popularity prediction. 上海交通大学计算机科学博零，入选博士生致远荣誉计划。研究方向包括大语言模型水印与安全、模型编辑的鲁棒性与不确定性，以及多模态流行度预测。

Before the PhD I completed a dual-degree undergraduate program in Computer Science and Finance at SJTU, selected for the university's Bole Program for outstanding undergraduates. 本科就读于上海交通大学计算机科学与金融双学位，入选"伯乐计划"上海交通大学卓越本科生培养计划。
